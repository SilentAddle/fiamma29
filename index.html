Schedule Changes

Monday, April 9th

Well, it's been three months, and as some of you know every three months or so my schedule takes a little change, we mix things up a little bit, drop what I'm not having so much fun playing, pick up something I am more interested in.
One of the notable changes to the new schedule, Saturdays are becoming a "What Fia Feels Like" day (or as we like to call it Waffle/WFFL), community games will still occur, but they will be moved off-stream so there can be a focus on the community.
I'm also planning to bring you some more Stardew, with the upcoming release of Multiplayer we may have ourselves a Fiamily Farm coming.

Twitch Brisbane Meetup

Saturday, April 7th

This coming Saturday, April 14th, the Brisbane based members of the Fiamily will be descending upon the Twitch-Brisbane meetup.
Be aware that the venue has limited capacity and entery will be on a first come first served basis, so if you want to hang out with us and a whole bunch of cool people, turn up early to avoid disappointment!
